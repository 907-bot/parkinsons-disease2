Feature Descriptions

Frequency Features

Measures of fundamental frequency (F0) which represent the base frequency of vocal vibrations.

Perturbation Features

Measures of variations in frequency (jitter) and amplitude (shimmer) of the voice signal.

Nonlinear Features

Complex measures derived from signal processing including entropy and correlation dimensions.

Harmonic Features

Ratios of harmonic to noise content in the voice, indicating voice quality.
